About ManageMe

ManageMe was created to bring calm, clarity and dignity to personal task management. Instead of noisy dashboards or endless configuration, you get a focused daily view, frictionless voice input, and an assistant that actually helps you finish—not just create—tasks.

We obsess over latency, accessibility, and readability. Every interaction seeks to reduce cognitive load so you can glide through planning and get back to life.

Our Principles

Speed, clarity, minimal friction. Every millisecond matters.

Privacy

Your data lives locally; you decide when to export or sync.

Assistive First

Accessible typography, high-contrast options, and voice.

Continuous Improvement

We iterate with user feedback to ship meaningful upgrades.
